Days 1–30

Learn & Orient

"Understand the team, tools, and how IBM works. Lay strong foundations for contribution."

  • Complete all mandatory compliance and security training
  • Meet your immediate team and key stakeholders
  • Understand IBM Future Now's mission and structure
  • Set up all required tools and access credentials
  • Shadow at least one live client engagement
  • Align on objectives and expectations with Sarah
  • Begin IBM w3 learning path for your role
  • Attend at least two community or network events
Days 31–60

Contribute & Connect

"Actively contribute to work and build your IBM network beyond the immediate team."

  • Take on a defined deliverable within a project
  • Expand your network beyond the immediate team
  • Identify and connect with a mentor
  • Present something to your team — even informally
  • Understand IBM's go-to-market and client positioning
  • Review career development frameworks with Sarah
  • Complete IBM Consulting core skills certification
  • Share one insight or piece of knowledge with the team
Days 61–90

Lead & Grow

"Operate independently, drive value, and set the trajectory for your IBM career."

  • Own a defined area of responsibility on a project
  • Contribute to a client or internal proposal
  • Identify one area to improve team processes
  • Complete your first formal review with Sarah
  • Set 6-month goals aligned to IBM priorities
  • Explore specialisation paths (AI, Cloud, Sustainability)
  • Join or lead a working group or community initiative
  • Provide structured feedback on your onboarding experience
